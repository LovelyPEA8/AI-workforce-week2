Step-by-Step Process in Creating Promise Career & Academic Agent Using Copilot Chat.

Step 1: Open Copilot

Go to Microsoft Copilot Chat

Step 2: Find “Agents”

After logging in:

* Look at the side menu.
* Find something called Agents.
* Click Create Agent or New Agent.

In this case, my AI assistant will help people with:

* Careers
* School work
* CVs
* Interviews
* Study advice

Step 3: Naming the Agent

* Promise Career & Academic Agent

Step 4: Telling the Agent What Its Job Is.

This agent helps students and job seekers with career advice, CV writing, interview preparation, study tips, and academic guidance.

Step 5: Telling the Agent How to Talk

* Friendly
* Professional
* Easy to understand
* Short and clear answers

Step 6: Add What the Agent Should Help With

* CV writing
* Interview questions
* Study timetable creation
* Scholarship advice
* Career planning
* Time management

Step 7: Add Rules

Told the AI what not to do.

* Don’t give fake job promises
* Don’t provide harmful advice
* Encourage users to check official school or job websites
(This is to keep the agent in line with its purpose)

Step 8: Create

* Click Create

Step 9: Testing the Agent

Asking questions to see how well it works.

Career Questions

* Help me write a CV
* Help me generate a CV template
* How do I prepare for an interview?
* What skills are needed in tech?

Academic Questions

* Create a reading timetable
* How can I study better?

Step 10: Improve the Agent

After testing:

* Checked if answers make sense
* Checked if responses are helpful
* Fixed anything confusing
