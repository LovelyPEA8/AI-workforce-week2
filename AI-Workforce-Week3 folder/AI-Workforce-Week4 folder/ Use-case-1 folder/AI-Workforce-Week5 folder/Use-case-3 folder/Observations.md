Observations About Promise Oy Employee Support Assistant Agent

1. What Prompts Worked Well

The agent worked best when asked clear and simple questions that matched the trigger phrases.

Examples of prompts that worked well:

* What are the office hours?
* How do I contact IT?
* Where is the employee portal?
* How do I access the company portal?

These worked well because the wording was very close to the trigger phrases created in the topics.

2. What Responses Were Accurate or Inaccurate

Accurate Responses

The agent gave correct answers when asked questions related to:

* Office opening and closing times
* IT support contact information
* Company portal access instructions

The responses were fast, direct, and easy to understand.

Inaccurate or Weak Responses

The agent struggled when:

* Questions were too different from the trigger phrases
* When asked follow-up questions
* When combined with multiple questions


3. Did the Agent Stay Within Its Purpose?

Yes, the agent mostly stayed within its purpose.

Its purpose was to:

* Answer employee FAQ questions
* Provide office information
* Help with IT contact details
* Guide users to the company portal

The assistant focused mainly on those tasks and did not try to answer unrelated questions.

4. What Should Be Improved

a) Add More Trigger Phrases

b) Add Better Fallback Responses

c) Add More Topics

d) Improve Conversation Flow

e) Add Real Company Resources

The assistant would work better if connected to:

* Employee databases
* Company knowledge base

That would allow it to provide live company information instead of only prewritten responses.
