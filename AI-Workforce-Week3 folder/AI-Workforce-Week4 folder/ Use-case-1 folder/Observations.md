Observations About the Career & Academics Agent 

1. What Prompts Worked Well

These were the kinds of questions the agent answered properly:
* I need a CV Template
* Help me write a CV
* How can I move into tech?
* What skills are needed for data entry jobs?

Why they worked well:
The questions were clear and directly related to careers or academics, which is the main purpose of the agent.

2. What Responses Were Accurate

The agent gave good responses when:

* Explaining study tips
* Suggesting interview preparation methods
* Helping with CV formatting
* Explaining beginner tech paths

These answers were useful and realistic.

3. What Responses Were Inaccurate or Weak

Some responses were not fully reliable or detailed enough.

Examples:
* Scholarship deadlines may be outdated
* Some job salary information may be incorrect
* Career advice may sound too general
* The AI may sometimes repeat information

4. Did the Agent Stay Within Its Purpose?

Mostly yes.

The agent stayed focused on:
* Careers
* Education
* CVs
* Interviews
* Study guidance

However, sometimes the AI may drift slightly if users ask unrelated questions.

5. What I would Improve

Some areas that can make the agent better:

a) More Human Responses

b) Shorter Answers

c) Better Accuracy

d) Stronger Focus

e) More Personalization

In Conclusion, The Career & Academics Agent worked well for basic career and school support. It was especially useful for CV help, interview preparation, study advice, and career guidance. However, the agent still needs improvements in accuracy, personalization, and making responses sound more natural and less robotic.
