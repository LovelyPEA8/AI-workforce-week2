The Career & Academic AI Companion is a smart assistant you can build using Microsoft Copilot Studio to help with school work and job-related tasks.
Many people find it hard to write a good CV, prepare cover letters, or understand their academic work, and this agent is designed to make those things easier.
It solves the problem of confusion, lack of guidance, and time pressure when handling career and academic responsibilities.

This agent is mainly for students, recent graduates, and anyone looking for a job or trying to improve their academic performance.
It is also helpful for people who are not sure how to present themselves professionally or don’t know what career path to follow.

The agent can help create simple and professional CVs.
It can write cover letters based on the job you want to apply for.
It can also help rewrite emails to sound more formal and clear.
If you have a long article or assignment, it can summarize it into easy-to-understand points.
It can explain difficult topics in a simple way.

You can ask it questions like “Help me write my CV,” “Explain this topic,” or “What jobs can I do with my degree?”
It can also suggest career options based on your skills and interests.
Overall, it acts like a personal helper that makes school and job preparation less stressful and more organized.
