

Copilot Free is like a basic AI chatbot you can use on the web or your phone.
It can answer questions, help write things, and generate images, but it mainly uses public internet data and isn’t deeply connected to your files.
Best for: casual users, students, or anyone just trying AI.

Copilot Pro is the paid “faster and stronger” version of the same thing.
You get quicker responses, priority access (so it works well even when busy), and it can help inside apps like Word or Excel if you already have Microsoft 365.
Best for: individuals who use AI a lot and want help with documents, presentations, or daily productivity.

Microsoft 365 Copilot is more like a workplace assistant built into your job tools.
It can read and use your emails, files, meetings, and chats to help you do work (like summarizing meetings or drafting reports based on your company data).
It also includes extra security and admin controls for businesses.
Best for: companies, teams, and professionals who need AI that works with their work data.

**In short:**

* Free = basic AI helper
* Pro = better, faster personal AI
* Microsoft 365 Copilot = full-on work assistant connected to your job

The main difference is how powerful it is and how deeply it connects to your files and apps.
